bananapudding

Making bananapudding can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics.

Ingredients

  • Milk and pudding mix
  • Sweetened condensed milk
  • Vanilla
  • Frozen whipped topping
  • Vanilla wafers
  • Bananas

Steps

  1. Whisk the pudding mix into the milk.
  2. Add the sweetened condensed milk and vanilla.
  3. MFold in the frozen whipped topping.
  4. Layer the mixture with the sliced bananas and vanilla wafers.
